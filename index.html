About the Program

A Dollar of Difference is a student-led micro-scholarship initiative designed to help peers in emergency financial need. Funded by small donations from fellow students, this program turns community care into real, life-changing support.

How to Donate

Want to make a difference? Just one dollar can go a long way. You can donate via:

  • Venmo: @DollarOfDifference
  • CashApp: $DollarOfDifference
  • PayPal: paypal.me/dollarofdifference

Application for Emergency Support

If you're a student facing an urgent financial need, apply below. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
